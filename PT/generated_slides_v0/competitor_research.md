# Competitor Research Notes

Used for slide 4 differentiation.

## Saramin AI mock interview
- Source: https://m.saramin.co.kr/member/ai-interview
- Observed positioning: resume-based customized questions, realistic AI interviewer conversation, follow-up questions and feedback.
- PathFiner differentiation used in slide: not only mock interview practice, but connecting company/job posting/self-introduction/profile evidence into a preparation roadmap.

## Teal AI Interview Practice
- Source: https://www.tealhq.com/tools/ai-interview-practice
- Observed positioning: AI Interview Agent analyzes saved job descriptions and creates role-specific practice sessions, with interview type selection and feedback/performance insights.
- PathFiner differentiation used in slide: more explicit evidence graph across company, job competency, posting requirements, and personal experience.

## Kickresume AI Job Interview Questions Generator
- Source: https://www.kickresume.com/en/ai-job-interview-questions-generator/
- Observed positioning: tailored interview questions and suggested answers based on CV, job title, and optional job description.
- PathFiner differentiation used in slide: turning expected questions into the target learning-depth standard and roadmap priorities.

## Google Interview Warmup
- Source: https://grow.google/interview-warmup-archived
- Observed positioning: users answer questions aloud, get real-time transcription and answer insights such as job-related terms and talking points.
- PathFiner differentiation used in slide: pre-interview analysis and GraphRAG-based recommendation before practice, rather than only answer insight after response.
